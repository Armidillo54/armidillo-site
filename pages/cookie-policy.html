What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how visitors use the site.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

  • Google Analytics opt-out: GA Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Browser cookie controls: Available in your browser's privacy/security settings
  • Do Not Track: We respect browser Do Not Track signals
