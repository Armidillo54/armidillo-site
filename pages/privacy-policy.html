Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 4, 2025  ·  Effective Date: August 4, 2025

This privacy policy is designed to comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable US state privacy laws.

Privacy Officer: info@armidillo.com

1. About This Policy

Armidillo ("we," "our," or "us") operates armidillo.com, a free educational resource about online safety, identity theft prevention, and fraud awareness. We collect minimal personal data and do not sell it to third parties.

2. Personal Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Newsletter signup: Email address for security tips and updates

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Analytics: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site (via Google Analytics)
  • Cookies: Session cookies and analytics cookies
  • Affiliate tracking: Click-through data for recommended security tools

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send requested security newsletters and updates
  • To analyze site performance and improve content
  • To track affiliate link performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics sets cookies to collect anonymized usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Services

We use affiliate links to services including Aura, NordVPN, Ledger, and Amazon. These services have their own privacy policies. We may earn a commission when you click these links at no additional cost to you.

6. Your Rights (PIPEDA / US State Laws)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Withdraw consent to email communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: info@armidillo.com

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. We do not store credit card numbers or sensitive financial information.
