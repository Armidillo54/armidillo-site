Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026  ·  Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Important: Armidillo is a US-focused educational resource. Our primary audience is US residents. This policy is designed to comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), the FTC Act, and applicable federal privacy requirements.

1. Who We Are

Armidillo ("we," "our," or "us") operates armidillo.com, a free educational resource about online safety, identity theft prevention, and fraud awareness for US consumers. We are not a law firm, financial advisor, or government agency.

Contact: info@armidillo.com

2. Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics data: IP address (anonymized), browser type, operating system, pages visited, time on site, referral source — collected via Google Analytics 4
  • Cookies: Session identifiers, analytics cookies, and affiliate tracking cookies (see our Cookie Policy for full details)
  • Affiliate click data: When you click affiliate links (e.g., to Aura), our affiliate partners may track which pages you visited and whether a purchase occurred
  • Log data: Standard server logs including your IP address, browser, and access times (via Cloudflare)

Information You Voluntarily Provide

We do not currently operate contact forms or email subscription services. If you email us at info@armidillo.com, we will receive your email address and any information you include in that message.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze site performance, understand how visitors use our content, and improve our educational resources
  • To track affiliate link performance and earn commissions that fund this free resource
  • To comply with our legal obligations
  • To respond to emails you send us

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

4. Google Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand site traffic. GA4 collects anonymized usage data through cookies. This data is processed by Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043).

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can also select "Essential Only" in our cookie banner to prevent analytics cookies from loading.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Affiliate Tracking

When you click affiliate links on our site (including links to Aura Identity Protection), our affiliate partners (currently Impact Radius/Aura) may set cookies or use pixel tracking to attribute purchases. This is necessary for us to earn affiliate commissions. See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.

7. Your Rights — California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the CCPA/CPRA:

  • Right to Know: You may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete your personal information (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. We do not sell or share personal data for advertising purposes
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by CPRA

To exercise your rights, email: info@armidillo.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

8. Your Rights — All US Users

  • Opt out of Google Analytics: Use the opt-out browser add-on
  • Limit analytics cookies: Click "Essential Only" in our cookie banner
  • Request information or deletion: Email info@armidillo.com

9. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months per our GA4 settings. Email correspondence is retained until no longer needed. We do not maintain large databases of personal information.

10. Data Security

Our site is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption) via Cloudflare. We do not store credit card numbers, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive financial information. Our site is static HTML and does not have a user login system.

11. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at info@armidillo.com immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
