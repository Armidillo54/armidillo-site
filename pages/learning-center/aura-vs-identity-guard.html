Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if my identity is stolen?

Immediately report the theft to your bank and credit card companies, place a fraud alert on your credit reports, and consider using an identity theft protection service like Aura.

How long does it take to recover from identity theft?

Recovery time can vary significantly based on the extent of the theft and your swift actions. It can take anywhere from a few hours to several months to resolve all issues.

Are identity theft protection services worth it?

For many people, the peace of mind and proactive monitoring that identity theft protection services provide are well worth the investment, especially if you have experienced identity theft in the past.

Can I monitor my credit without a service?

Yes, you can monitor your credit for free through annual credit reports and other tools, but these services may not provide real-time alerts or comprehensive protection that dedicated services like Aura offer.
