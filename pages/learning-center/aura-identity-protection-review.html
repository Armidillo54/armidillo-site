What is Aura?

Aura is a comprehensive identity protection service designed to safeguard your personal information and help you recover in the event of identity theft. It offers features like credit monitoring, identity theft insurance, and alerts for suspicious activity, making it a robust solution for individuals concerned about fraud. Aura also provides user-friendly tools and resources for understanding and managing your identity.

Key Features of Aura

  1. Credit Monitoring

Aura monitors your credit reports at all three major bureaus (Experian, TransUnion, and Equifax) 24/7. You’ll receive alerts for any changes to your credit report, helping you catch potential fraud early.

  1. Identity Theft Insurance

In the unfortunate event that your identity is stolen, Aura offers up to $1 million in identity theft insurance. This coverage helps with expenses related to identity recovery, such as legal fees and lost wages.

  1. Dark Web Monitoring

Aura scans the dark web for your personal information, such as Social Security numbers, credit card information, and email addresses. If your data is found, you’ll receive immediate alerts.

  1. Identity Restoration Services

Aura provides expert assistance if your identity is compromised. Their dedicated restoration specialists will work with you to resolve any issues and restore your identity.

  1. User-Friendly App

The Aura app is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate. It allows you to manage your account, view alerts, and access resources all in one place.

  1. Family Plans

Aura offers family plans that cover multiple members, providing peace of mind for households concerned about identity theft.
