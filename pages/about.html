Our Mission

Cyber fraud is the fastest-growing crime category on earth. Global fraud losses hit an estimated $442 billion in 2025, with INTERPOL reporting a 54% rise in fraud-related Notices and Diffusions from 2024 to 2025. When someone loses $100,000 to a crypto scam, the first thing they do is search for help. Armidillo exists to be there when they do.

We deliver genuinely helpful guidance backed by real investigative expertise, and we help the most at-risk users find the protection tools they need. Our content is written by the Armidillo Team — a group of researchers and investigators focused on the fraud prevention space. We publish under an editorial team byline to protect operational security while maintaining the highest editorial standards.
