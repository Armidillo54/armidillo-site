Family Protection Plans

Protect your entire family — including aging parents and children — with one identity protection plan. Best family plans in 2026 reviewed and compared.

Why Family Plans Matter

Identity theft doesn't target individuals in isolation. A family data breach can compromise children's Social Security Numbers (which often go undetected for years), elderly parents' Medicare information, and adult members' financial accounts simultaneously. Family plans provide comprehensive coverage at significantly lower per-person costs than individual subscriptions.

What to Look For in a Family Plan

  • Unlimited children's coverage — child identity theft is severely underreported
  • Coverage for multiple adults — protect both spouses and aging parents
  • Insurance per adult member, not per household — individual coverage amounts matter
  • Easy family member onboarding — should take minutes, not hours
  • Simple unified dashboard to monitor the whole family
