Buyer's Guide: PoE vs Wireless

✅ Choose PoE/Wired If...

  • You own your home and can run Ethernet cable
  • You want 24/7 continuous recording (not motion-triggered clips)
  • You want maximum reliability with no interference
  • You have 4+ cameras planned

✅ Choose Wireless If...

  • You rent or can't run cables
  • You need 1–3 cameras in specific spots
  • You want quick DIY installation

What You'll Need for Installation

  • PoE NVR system (network video recorder)
  • Cat5e or Cat6 Ethernet cable (measure your runs)
  • 2–4TB hard drive for the NVR
  • Power drill and cable clips for routing
  • A router with a free port, or a PoE switch
