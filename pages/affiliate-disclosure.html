1. What Is an Affiliate Relationship?

Armidillo is a free educational resource. To keep this site running at no cost to you, we participate in affiliate marketing programs. When you click certain links on our site and subsequently make a purchase or take a qualifying action, we may receive a financial commission from the company. This is called an "affiliate commission."

Important: You pay the same price whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the company's website. Our commissions come from the company's marketing budget — not from any markup on your purchase price.
